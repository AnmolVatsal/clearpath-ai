# Problem Statement

Healthcare revenue cycle management faces inefficiencies due to fragmented data systems, complex payer policies, and limited transparency in decision-making.

Key challenges include:
- Long authorization timelines (up to 14 days)
- High claim denial rates (~40%)
- Lack of explainability in outcomes
- Manual, error-prone workflows

A unified, auditable, and automated decision system is required to improve speed, accuracy, and compliance.
